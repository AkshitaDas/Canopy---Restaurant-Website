Why Us

Why Choose Our Restaurant

01

Design & Detailing

The restaurant is an exquisite ode to Bengal’s timeless architecture and design. It celebrates distinctive architectural elements and nuances visible in many iconic buildings of Kolkata and the region.

02

Cuisine

Bengal has been the melting pot of cultures from across India & overseas and offers a wealth of cuisines. The restaurant celebrates the culinary heritage of the region while also offering delectable global cuisines to gourmet connoisseurs.

03

Sustainability

The objective always is to have a purely positive impact on the planet and the community while delivering authentic indigenous experiences.
